ABOUT US

Vidya Vihar Residential School is a fully residential institution for boys (Grades IV to XII) and girls (Grade VI onwards), and the first ISO 9001-certified school in Bihar. Spanning 34 acres, it provides a serene, green campus with modern facilities, including academic buildings, hostels, an auditorium, a healthcare center, and staff quarters. The school goes beyond CBSE standards with advanced science and computer labs, a well-stocked library, an Atal Tinkering Lab (ATL), and comprehensive sports and arts facilities. With a strong focus on AI-driven education, we equip students with future-ready skills, empowering them to innovate, adapt, and excel in an ever-evolving digital world.
